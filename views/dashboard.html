What is SymptHome?

  • Disease Feedback

    The purpose of our webiste is to provide you with disease feedback based on your input in terms of symptoms. We will compare them against any disease in our database and try to give you accurate results!

  • Save Time

    These days, life is extremely fast for everyone. It is rare to find time for yourself. Our website gives you the ability to monitor your health and check potentially dangerous symptoms in a matter of seconds!

    Avoid making time consuming appointments with your local doctors, and waiting in queues. We will serve you instantly!

  • Save Money

    Our website is completely free of charge and our only goal is to improve overall health and well-being of the human race by using the power of information technologies!

  • Avoid Human Error

    There are millions of diseases out there and no human can possibly remember all of them so it is expected that even the experienced doctors can make mistakes when diagnosing diseases and can point you in the wrong direction. This webpage will check every single possibility without forgetting and will do so extremely fast!

  • Medicine Recommendations
